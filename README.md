xSalice Dungeon/General Reaction Profiles

I've done my best here to create profiles designed for casual play.
No more blowing your 2 minute burst on a boss with 1% HP left!
No more having to turn off damage over time abilities yourself on your profile when there's 9 enemies around you.
No more having to worry about your mitigations either never getting used, or getting used improperly.

These reactions should work well in all daily roulettes, treasure maps, fates, overworld combat, and what ever else you're doing that isn't raiding. 

*THESE ARE FOR GENERAL USE ONLY*
If you're looking for raid profiles, or timelines, please check out the archekilliox profiles that are hosted over on ekilliox's repository! We collaborate on the raid profiles to bring our best directly to you as quickly & accurately. 

Please visit the link below for our raid focused reaction profiles!

There are at least 3 toggles for each class:
-Mitigation
    (uses every part of your classes mitigation kit on incoming raidwide attacks)
-Variable Holds
    (holds your cooldowns to ensure 2min bursts before the boss dies)
-QOL Tweaks 
    (things like out of combat sprint)
